About

Hi, I’m Matthew

I’m a photographer and educator based in Dallas, Texas, who believes photography is less about perfection and more about honoring what’s real. My work leans warm, nostalgic, and people-first — the kind of images that still feel true when you pull them out of a shoebox years from now.

Since 2017, I’ve photographed hundreds of portraits, couples, live music, and events, always with the same goal: help people feel seen, not staged into someone else’s trend.

If you’re looking for stiff poses and retouched perfection, I’m probably not your photographer. If you want images that feel like you, I’d love to work together.
