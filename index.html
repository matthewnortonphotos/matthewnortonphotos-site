About

Hi, I’m Matthew

I’m a photographer and educator based in Dallas, Texas, who believes photography is less about perfection and more about honoring what’s real. My work leans warm, nostalgic, and people-first — the kind of images that still feel true when you look back years from now.

Since 2017, I’ve photographed hundreds of portraits, couples, live music, and events, always with the same goal: help people feel seen with dignity and intention, not staged into someone else’s trend.

When I’m not shooting, I’m writing and teaching other creatives how to stay deeply curious behind the lens, and spending time with my wife, Hanna, and our daughter, Birdie — my most patient test subjects.

If you’re looking for stiff poses and airbrushed perfection, I’m probably not your photographer. If you want images that feel like you, I’d love to work together.
