About

Who’s behind the camera

I’m Matthew Norton, a Dallas-based photographer who cares more about how a moment lives in your memory than how perfect it looks on a screen.

Most of my work lives in the worlds of portraits, brands, events, and street photography—real people, real light, real expressions. I’ll give you direction when you need it and disappear into the background when you don’t.

My goal is simple: images that feel like you were there, not like you were performing. Whether it’s a brand refresh, a creative project, or a once-in-a-lifetime moment, I’m here to help you remember it well.

When I’m not behind the camera, I’m usually nerding out over prints, nostalgic cameras, or working with creative brands that care about story just as much as aesthetic.

What I shoot

  • Portraits Individuals, creatives, musicians, founders
  • Couples Engagements, anniversaries, just-because sessions
  • Brands Small businesses, personal brands, campaigns
  • Events Conferences, worship nights, live gatherings
